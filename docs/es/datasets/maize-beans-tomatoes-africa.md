## Citación

Citas originales de la publicación del dataset:

This dataset comprises images from 10 individual datasets collected in various countries of Sub-Saharan Africa. It has a total of 68,519 images in 32 classes. It comprises 2 datasets for beans (one from Uganda [1] and another from Tanzania [2]), and 5 datasets for maize (each from Burkina Faso [3], Uganda [4] Namibia [5], and two from Tanzania [5] and [6]), and 3 datasets for Tomatoes (each from Burkina Faso [3], Ghana [5], and Nigeria [6]). This collection attempts to solve the problem of geographical diversity for agricultural datasets in the region and hence lead to the development of resilient Computer Vision applications for detecting and classifying crop diseases.
